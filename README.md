## About Medserve
- Our app streamlines access to essential services by addressing long waiting times, and missed updates. Users can create and manage their accounts securely, join online queues, and monitor their position in real time, reducing unnecessary on-site waiting. The app provides a clear schedule of services and doctor availability, allowing users to plan appointments efficiently. Real-time notifications keep users informed about upcoming appointments and important announcements. By integrating these features into a single platform, our solution enhances convenience, improves communication, and ensures a smoother, more organized experience for users seeking these services.
